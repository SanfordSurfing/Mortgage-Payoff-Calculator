提供两个计算器，让用户在不同条件下使用对应的计算器。

计算器1：If you know the remaining loan term
    描述：Use this calculator if the term length of the remaining loan is known and there is information on the original loan – good for new loans or preexisting loans that have never been supplemented with any external payments.
    参数：
        original loan amount
        original loan term (years)
        intereste rate (%)
        remaining term  _years _ months
        repayment options:
            payback together
            repayment with extra payments:
                $_ permonth
                $_ per year
                $_ one time
            biweekly repayment
            normal repayment
    计算结果展示：
        summary: payoff in _ years and _ months
        节省的利息和时间，对比原方案。柱状图形式、表格形式、折线图形式。


计算器2：If you don't know the remaining loan term
    描述：Use this calculator if the term length of the remaining loan is not known. The unpaid principal balance, interest rate, and monthly payment values can be found in the monthly or quarterly mortgage statement.
    参数：
        unpaid principal balance
        monthly payment
        intereste rate (%)
        repayment options:
            repayment with extra payments:
                $_ permonth
                $_ per year
                $_ one time
            biweekly repayment
            normal repayment
    计算结果展示：
        summary: payoff in _ years and _ months
        节省的利息和时间，对比原方案。柱状图形式、表格形式、折线图形式。
